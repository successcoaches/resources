What to Know

Graduation Requirements (Standard 14–18 Program)

  • 4 credits English
  • 3 credits Math
  • 3 credits Science
  • 3.5 credits Social Studies
  • 8 credits Elective (including PE/Health and Fine Art minimums)
  • Up to 75% of the credits required for graduation may transfer from a previous school — that's 16 credits on the 21.5-credit track, or 18 credits on the 24-credit track
